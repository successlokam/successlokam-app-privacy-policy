This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Successlokam Quiz mobile application (the "Service").

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from you directly. Our app, Successlokam Quiz, is designed to be used without requiring you to provide personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Our Service uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Below are the services we use:

    Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, and app usage data to provide personalized ads. We do not have access to or control over this data collection.

    You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services by visiting their policy page: Google Ad Policy.

    Our app uses Firebase Cloud Firestore to download and display the quiz questions and related content. We use this service in a read-only capacity. The app does not send any of your personal information or device data to our Firebase database.

    For more information, you can review the Firebase privacy policy: Firebase Privacy and Security.

The security of your data is important to us. The data for the quiz questions is retrieved over a secure connection (HTTPS). However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
