Privacy Policy

For Successlokam Quiz App

Effective Date: June 16, 2025

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of data when you use our Successlokam Quiz mobile application (the "Service").

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from you directly. Our app, Successlokam Quiz, is designed to be used without requiring you to provide personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Automatically Collected Data (Non-Personal)

While we do not collect personal data, our app uses third-party services that may collect anonymous information to serve advertisements and ensure the app functions correctly.

Third-Party Services

Our Service uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Below are the services we use:

  • Google AdMob:

    Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use data such as your device's advertising ID and app usage to provide personalized ads. We do not have access to or control over this data collection. You can learn more by visiting their policy page: Google Ad Policy.

  • Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore):

    Our app uses Firebase Cloud Firestore to download quiz questions. The app does not send any of your personal or device data to our Firebase database. For more information, you can review the Firebase privacy policy: Firebase Privacy and Security.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Quiz data is retrieved over a secure connection (HTTPS). However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.
